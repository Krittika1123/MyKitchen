Mango Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Chopped Mangoes
  • 1 Cup Cold Milk
  • 4 Tbsp Sugar
  • 3-4 Ice Cubes

Method

  • Chop 2 mangoes and add it into the grinder it should be 2 cups.
  • Add a cup of milk to the grinder.
  • Add 4tbsp of sugar.
  • Add 3-4 ice cubes.
  • Then grind the mixture.
  • You will know when it is finished when it becomes like a creamy texture thick with no bits.
  • Then mix with a spoon
  • Pour the mixture into a glass

Mango Milkshake is ready to be served!

Enjoy!
