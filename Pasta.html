Masala Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta (any variety)- 1.5 cup
  • sufficient water to boil
  • Oil- 1 tbsp
  • Butter-1 tbsp
  • Chopped Ginger - 1 tbsp
  • Chopped Garlic- 1 tbsp
  • Chopped Onions- 1/2 cup
  • Tomato Puree- 1 cup
  • Chopped Carrots- 1/4 cup
  • Chopped Green chillies - 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder- 1 tsp
  • Red chilli Powder- 1 tsp (optional)
  • Coriander powder- 1 tsp
  • Salt
  • Mixed herbs & oregano (optional)

Method

  • Add sufficent water to a pot.
  • Bring it to a boil.
  • Add 1tsp of salt.
  • Add the pasta of your choice I chose spiral 1.5 cup
  • Boil for 10-12 mins.
  • Strain pasta & keep it to a side.
  • In a different pan add 1tbsp of oil
  • Add 1tbsp of butter.
  • Add 1tbsp of chopped garlic and ginger.
  • Add 1/2 a cup of chopped oinions.
  • Give it stir.
  • Add 1 chopped green chili.
  • Add 1 cup of tomato puree.
  • Add 1tsp of turmeric powder.
  • Add 1 tsp of red chili powder.
  • Add 1tsp of coriander powder.
  • Add 2 pinches of salt.
  • Give it a mix.
  • Cover & cook until oil spreads out.
  • Add the boiled pasta.
  • Add the mixed herbs and oregano.
  • Mix well.

Masala Pasta is ready to be served!

Enjoy!
