Red Dal

Ingredients

  • red lentils 1 cup (200 grams)
  • oil 2 tbsp
  • whole cumin seeds 1 tsp
  • curry leaves 4-5 count
  • finely chopped onion 1 medium size
  • garlic-ginger paste 1 tsp
  • slitted green chilies 4 count (optional)
  • salt 1 heaped tsp
  • coriander powder 1/2 tsp
  • red chili powder 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
  • chopped tomatoes 2 medium size
  • chopped coriander leaves 2 tbsp

Method

  • Wash red dal thoroughly.
  • Medium heat up pan.
  • Add 2tbsp of oil.
  • Add 1tbsp of cumin seeds.
  • Add curry leaves about 2-3 pieces.
  • Add the finely chopped onion. Onion should be a medium sized onion.
  • Mix for 5 minutes.
  • Add 1tsp of ginger garlic paste to the mix.
  • Add green chilies 4 small ones if using.
  • Add 1 tsp of salt.
  • Add coriander powder and red chili powder 1/2 tsp. If you don't want to add any chilies then you don't need to the chilies give a spicy favlour.
  • Add 1/4 tsp of turmeric powder.
  • Give it another mix. Remember to mix while adding the ingredients for example after adding ginger garlic paste give it a mix.
  • Add 2 medium sized chopped tomatoes.
  • Add the red dal.
  • Fry the lentils for 2 minutes.
  • Add 4 hot cup of water adjust the amount of water to your preference.
  • Let it boil for 3-5 minutes
  • Cover the pan until lentils are tender.
  • After about 15 minutes. Whisk till the lentils are broken..
  • Add 2tbsp of chopped coriander.

Red Dal is ready to be served!

Enjoy!
