Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken - 200 gms
  • Pound Ginger & Garlic - 1 tsp
  • Yoghurt - 2 tsp
  • Lemon juice Of 1/2 Lemon
  • Chili Powder - 2 tsp (optional)
  • Garam Masala - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Onion - 2 (chopped)
  • Pound Ginger & Garlic - 1 tsp
  • Tomato - 3 nos. (chopped)
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Cumin Powder - 1 tsp
  • Coriander Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Water - 3/4 cup
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Fresh Cream - 1/4 cup
  • Methi
  • Coriander Leaves

Method

  • To a bowl, add the boneless chicken pieces, pound ginger garlic, yoghurt, lemon juice, red chili powder, garam masala and salt
  • Mix everything well, and marinate the chicken for about 30 minutes
  • Heat a pan and add oil
  • Fry the marinated chicken pieces mixture till they are cooked and keep it aside
  • In a pan heat oil and butter, add onions and saute them till they are transparent
  • Add the pound ginger garlic and mix it well. Then add tomatoes saute them.
  • Add kashmiri red chilies and saute.
  • Add salt, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder. Add some water and cook till the tomatoes are mushy.
  • Then add cashewnuts and saute for 5 mins. Cool down the cooked masala and grind it to a fine paste by adding water.
  • Heat the same pan, add butter, red chili powder and keep the flame on low. Add the ground puree to the pan. Saute and add water.
  • Add the fried chicken pieces and mix it well. Add fresh cream mix it and simmer the gravy for about 10 minutes by closing the kadai.
  • Finally, add crushed kasuri methi and coriander leaves and mix well

Butter chicken is ready to be served!

Enjoy!
